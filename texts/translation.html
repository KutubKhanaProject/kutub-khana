Translation Work

Welcome to the Translation Work section of The Maktaba Project. This space is dedicated to making Urdu literary works accessible to a broader audience through high-quality translations.

We are actively working on translating classic and contemporary Urdu texts into other languages and vice versa. These translations aim to bridge cultural and linguistic barriers while preserving the essence of the original works.

If you're interested in contributing to our translation efforts, whether as a translator, editor, or reviewer, please Contact Us.
