About The Maktaba Project

Welcome to The Maktaba Project, a digital library dedicated to preserving and sharing Urdu literature. Our mission is to create a platform where readers, scholars, and enthusiasts can access timeless works of Urdu poetry, prose, and translations.

Our Vision

We aim to build a freely accessible digital archive that ensures the preservation of cultural heritage while fostering new creative contributions in Urdu literature.

Join Us

Whether you're an author, translator, or reader, we welcome your contributions and feedback. Help us grow and improve this literary treasure trove.
