About The Maktaba Project

Welcome to The Maktaba Project, a dedicated digital library preserving and sharing Urdu literature. Our mission is to provide free and open access to a wide array of Urdu texts, including poetry, prose, essays, and translations. We aim to build a resourceful platform where readers, researchers, and enthusiasts can explore and enjoy the richness of Urdu language and literature.

Our library is constantly growing, thanks to contributions from writers, translators, and volunteers from around the world. Whether you're here to read, contribute, or simply explore, we're thrilled to have you with us on this journey.

For inquiries or contributions, feel free to reach out through our Contact Page.
