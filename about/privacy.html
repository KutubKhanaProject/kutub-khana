Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how The Maktaba Project manages data and interactions with visitors.

1. Information We Collect

- Personal Information: The Maktaba Project does not actively collect personal data from users unless you voluntarily provide it via our contact form or email.
- Automatic Data Collection: Basic non-identifiable information, such as browser type and general website traffic analytics, may be collected to help improve user experience.

2. Use of Information

Any information voluntarily shared with us (e.g., via email or the contact form) will only be used for:

3. Cookies and Analytics

We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes. Minimal website analytics may be gathered (e.g., through server logs) to monitor website performance and user engagement.

4. Data Sharing

- We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.
- Any personal information provided via contact forms or emails will remain confidential.

Our website may contain links to third-party platforms (e.g., external archives, repositories). We are not responsible for their privacy practices, and we encourage you to review their privacy policies before interacting with them.
